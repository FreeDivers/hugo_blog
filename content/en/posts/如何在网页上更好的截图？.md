## F12 Command

Press F12 to open DevTools, or right-click the page and choose "Inspect".

![](https://fultal.qinyan.org/68747470733a2f2f747661312e73696e61696d672e636e2f6c617267652f303038754f5a454567793168356a3161717964707a6a3331676d3074693471702e6a7067.jpg)

Press `Ctrl+Shift+P`, type `Screenshot`, then choose the capture mode you want. After a short wait, the screenshot is downloaded automatically.

![](https://fultal.qinyan.org/68747470733a2f2f747661312e73696e61696d672e636e2f6c617267652f303038754f5a454567793168356a316d63757770796a333067713063303078722e6a7067.jpg)

## FireShot Plugin

Some people may say: "Your method above is still not fast enough. I need to open this, press that, type this... not convenient."

That is easy to solve: install a plugin. FireShot works very well.

> #### Download Links
>
> [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/take-webpage-screenshots/mcbpblocgmgfnpjjppndjkmgjaogfceg?hl=zh-cn)
>
> [Edge Add-ons](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/жЌ•жЌ‰зЅ‘йЎµж€Єе›ѕ-fireshotзљ„/fcbmiimfkmkkkffjlopcpdlgclncnknm?hl=zh-CN)
>
> [Firefox Extension](https://addons.mozilla.org/zh-CN/firefox/addon/fireshot/)

After installing FireShot, find and open it from the browser menu at the top right.

![](https://fultal.qinyan.org/68747470733a2f2f747661312e73696e61696d672e636e2f6c617267652f303038754f5a454567793168356a32647371366a706a333063333061737134792e6a7067.jpg)

Now choose the screenshot mode you need.

After choosing a mode, a save window will pop up. You can choose how to save the screenshot there.

![](https://fultal.qinyan.org/68747470733a2f2f747661312e73696e61696d672e636e2f6c617267652f303038754f5a454567793168356a3330706f61756d6a33307a663073726b36732e6a7067.jpg)

> #### About FireShot Paid Features
>
> The basic version is free and enough for screenshot capture. If you want to edit or annotate screenshots with FireShot's built-in editor, there is a FireShot Pro upgrade option in the save window. I do not recommend upgrading: those extra functions can usually be done with built-in system tools. FireShot Pro has a 10-day free trial, then costs around 60 USD.
